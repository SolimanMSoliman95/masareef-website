Everything in one place

A finance app that does the typing for you.

01Automatic capture

Bank SMS becomes transactions. No typing.

The moment your bank texts you, Masareef reads it, categorises it, and files it. Merchant, amount, category — sorted before you even open the app. You just live your life.

02AI budgeting

A money-buddy that actually helps — and roasts you a little.

Chat in Egyptian Arabic or English. Masareef builds a real budget from how you actually spend, nudges you before you overspend, and isn't afraid to call out that third coffee. Friendly, never preachy.

03Insights

Insights that make sense for how Egypt gets paid.

Spending by category, month-over-month trends, and a view that understands the salary cycle — so the end of the month never sneaks up on you again.

04Bills & subscriptions

Never get surprised by a renewal again.

Masareef spots your recurring bills and subscriptions in your SMS — electricity, mobile, streaming, the gym — and reminds you before they hit. See what renews, when, and for how much.

05Transfers & InstaPay

Every transfer, InstaPay and wallet — one timeline.

Vodafone Cash, InstaPay, bank transfers and Fawry all land in the same clean feed. Money in, money out, who and when — finally in one place you can actually read.

How it works

Connect

2

Understand

Encrypted on your phone. Backed up securely. Never sold.

Your data is encrypted on your phone with SQLCipher and backed up to your own secure account. We process your bank SMS to turn them into transactions, and our analytics is anonymous — no amounts, no merchant names. And we never sell your data.
